  • About Me!

    I’m Mia, and I am a versatile graphic designer, creative thinker, and illustrator from North Vancouver, BC whose secret ambition is to combine all my passions into one. I continuously strive to create clever ideas and designs that make for thoughtful solutions and for people to take something meaningful away, whether that be a smile or a spur of deep thought. I am also an optimist and will forever watch for and listen to every perspective that comes my way!
